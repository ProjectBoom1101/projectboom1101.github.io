What is the OpenCWC Project?

The OpenCWC Project was designed to provide the community with life-like CGI recreations of popular lolcows. It all began when a few young animators got together and thought about pranking Chris with a version of him from the mythical Dimension C-197. After a successful test animation, followed by a short film, we got to work recreating other lolcows and those associated with them, such as Chance Wilkins and Music Biz Marty.

We intend to keep the characters and other assets updated regularly with new technologies as we upskill in the 3D animation industry. If you have any suggestions or bugs you think we should know about, we welcome your feedback! Otherwise, scroll down to see what we have to offer.
